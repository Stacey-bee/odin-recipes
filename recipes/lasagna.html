Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is made with a very easy meat sauce as the base. The sauce is layered with noodles and cheese and then baked until bubbly.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of extre virgin oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow)
  • 2 cloves of minced garic
  • 28-ounce can of good quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces of tomato paste
  • 14 ounce can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup of sugar
  • Salt
To assemble the lasagna:
  • 1/2 pound of dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 24 ounces of mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 4 ounces of freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
